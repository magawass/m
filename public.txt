Magawa Students portal is now operational. 
Parents, Guardians and Students can view exam results through the Results portal on the menu.
Here is how to go about it:
1-Go to the Results Portal
2-Select Form 1, 2, 3 or 4 by clicking against the Form.
3-Enter students exam number in the text box for Exam Numbers.
4-Hit enter, you are done.
